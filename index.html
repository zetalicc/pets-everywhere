Our Services

Boarding Facility

All pet suites are climate controlled. All suites are custom painted, private, spacious, and luxurious for the elderly or pampered pooches to make them feel like king or queen. We offer various sized suites to fulfill your pet's needs.

Grooming

Grooming services include bathing, trimming and/or shaving to coat, nail clipping, teeth brushing and ear cleaning. Our professional groomers have extensive knowledge about different dog breeds.

Training

We offer a variety of exciting training options for you and your dog! All of our training techniques are scientifically grounded in Applied Behaviorism. Our methods are reward-based and always dog-friendly.

Dedicated Staff

Our trained professionals have extensive experience in pet care delivered with love. We have exceptional veterinarians available 24/7 to respond to your pet's medical needs.
